Technology

JRP Apps

JRP Apps is the technology division of JRP Industries. It develops mobile applications for consumers as well as software systems for business operations.

The current app portfolio includes Calculator Pro, Money Mate, Arrow Escape, Fit Mate, Chess Master and Water Sort. Beyond consumer products, the division also works on enterprise applications such as production and management systems.

  • Android and cross-platform mobile applications
  • Business automation and internal management software
  • Future SaaS and AI-assisted products

Textiles

JRP Sarees

JRP Sarees reflects the group’s manufacturing roots. The business is involved in saree and powerloom production and is building a stronger presence across wholesale, retail and dealer channels.

Its digital platform is intended to present collections professionally, support dealer relationships and make product discovery easier without losing the personal nature of the saree trade.

  • Powerloom saree manufacturing
  • Wholesale and retail business opportunities
  • Dealer network and digital collection showcase

Consumer goods

JRP Oils

JRP Oils is an emerging business focused on edible oils used by households and commercial customers. The initial product direction includes groundnut, sunflower, soyabean and mustard oils.

The division is being planned around reliable sourcing, consistent product standards, clear presentation and wholesale distribution capability.

  • Groundnut, sunflower, soyabean and mustard oil
  • Wholesale and distribution-led growth
  • Quality, traceability and customer confidence
