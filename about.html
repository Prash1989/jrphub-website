About us

A business group being built through experience, curiosity and steady execution.

JRP Industries is the parent company for a growing portfolio of technology, textile and consumer ventures.

Our story

From hands-on operations to a broader business platform.

The JRP journey combines practical experience in manufacturing with a strong interest in technology and business improvement. That combination led to the creation of individual products and ventures—and eventually to JRP Industries as the group identity connecting them.

Today, the group is developing three focused areas: JRP Apps for software and digital products, JRP Sarees for textile manufacturing and trade, and JRP Oils for edible oil products and distribution.

We are still at an early stage of the wider journey. That is why our priority is to establish strong fundamentals: useful products, clear processes, dependable service and honest communication.
