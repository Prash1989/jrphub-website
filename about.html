About JRP Industries

Built on ambition. Guided by integrity.

JRP Industries is a diversified business platform focused on creating trusted, future-ready companies.

Our story

A parent company designed for meaningful growth.

JRP Industries was formed to unify a growing portfolio of businesses under one purpose: build dependable products, apply technology intelligently and create long-term value for customers, partners and communities.

Our journey spans software, mobile applications, saree manufacturing, powerloom operations and edible oils. Each division has its own market focus while sharing common standards for integrity, quality and execution.
