About JRP Industries

One group. Different capabilities. A shared standard.

JRP Industries was created as the parent identity for businesses developed by the JRP team. Instead of treating each venture as an isolated project, we build common foundations around technology, operations, customer service and responsible growth.

Our current work spans software applications, saree manufacturing and the development of an edible oils business. Each division has its own market and operating model, while sharing the same emphasis on usefulness, quality and trust.
