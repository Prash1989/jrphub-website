Privacy commitment

Useful products should respect the people who use them.

JRP Apps follows a privacy-first development approach. We aim to collect only information that is necessary for a clearly explained product function, keep local features available where practical and avoid unnecessary device permissions.

When an application can provide value through offline or on-device processing, that is preferred over adding avoidable account, tracking or cloud dependencies. Product-specific privacy information is made available through the official JRP Apps website.
